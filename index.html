Tane R. Wood is a college student in the multimedia Production course at Lethbridge College. In high school, he was part of the robotics club which competed nationally and was the projections manager for a Play. In his spare time, he loves to do 3D animation and video production. He has an interest in making engaging stories with his animations. He has volunteered for Arts in the Parks to do the filming and set up for them. He also has an interest in game design and programming, while in high school making a short game.

He has an interest in anything electronic from having an interest in how things go together and take things apart to see how they were built, much to his mother's chagrin. Middle school is where he first joined a robotics club; this is where he gained much experience exploring little robots and the old computer equipment that the school had lying around. Later in high school he competed in tournaments and was a valuable teammate for the team. Also, in high school, he was in a play production where he ran and made the projections for the background. When introduced to new technologies he learns quickly and understands new concepts well.
